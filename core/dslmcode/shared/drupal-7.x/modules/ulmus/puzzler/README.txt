Puzzler makes it easy to insert bits and pieces of Wikipedia content into Drupal, keeping the information fresh as improvements are done by the community of Wikipedia editors.

Drupal project page is at https://www.drupal.org/project/puzzler
Installation, demo and tips are at http://www.vacilando.org/article/puzzler
If you encounter a problem or need support, see https://www.drupal.org/project/issues/puzzler
